Maochuan Lu - CS180 Project 1

What I do?

In this project, we aligned RGB plates in order to combine them and produce color images with as few artifacts as possible. To do this, we use a multiscale pyramid approach. We recurse the scale of our image down until we reach a target threshold of image size, at which point we iterate over a defined range of possible x and y offsets and calculate an accuracy score on each one at this level.
